JC Stock

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 5, 2026

Overview

JC Stock ("the App") is a fully offline inventory management application. We are committed to protecting your privacy. The App does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. All data you enter is stored exclusively on your own device and is never uploaded to any server.

Data We Do Not Collect

JC Stock does not collect any of the following:

  • Name, email address, or other identity information
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Crash reports or diagnostic data sent to our servers
  • Any inventory, item, or business data you enter in the App

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

All information you create in the App — including warehouses, items, stock records, categories, suppliers, and images — is stored locally on your device using an embedded database (Hive). This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not accessible to us or any third party
  • Is permanently deleted when you uninstall the App

Device Permissions

The App may request the following device permissions. Each permission is used solely for the stated purpose and for no other reason:

Permission Purpose Required?
Camera Scan barcodes and QR codes to quickly locate or add inventory items Optional
Photo Library Select images from your photo library to attach to inventory items Optional
Local Network Used solely to verify your in-app subscription status via RevenueCat; no personal data is transmitted Optional
Notifications Send local low-stock alert notifications when inventory falls below your set threshold Optional

You can grant or revoke any of these permissions at any time in your device Settings. Revoking a permission only disables the related feature; all other App functionality remains unaffected.

In-App Purchases & Subscriptions

JC Stock offers optional premium features via in-app subscriptions processed through Apple App Store. All payment transactions are handled entirely by Apple. We do not receive or store your payment details. Subscription status verification is performed by RevenueCat, whose privacy policy governs their data handling. Only an anonymous app user ID is used for this purpose — no personal information is involved.

Third-Party Services

The App integrates with the following third-party service solely for subscription management:

  • RevenueCat — Subscription status management. See their Privacy Policy.

No analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs are included in the App.

Data Security

Because all data remains on your device, the security of your inventory data depends on the security of your device. We recommend using a device passcode or biometric lock. If you use the App's export feature (CSV / Excel), the exported files are saved to your device's file system or shared via the system share sheet — please handle exported files with appropriate care.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, the "Last updated" date at the top of this page will be revised. Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
